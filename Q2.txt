web3 is considered to be third generation of internet. The major difference
between web2 and web3 is that web3 is decentralized whereas web2 is is 
centralized. web3 enables users to own their data and choose what to do
with it whereas in web2 centralized authorities have full control over
users data. web3 will help increase security of internet as web3 is
built on top of blockchian technology. web3 enables peer to peer interactions
without the need for intermediaries.
